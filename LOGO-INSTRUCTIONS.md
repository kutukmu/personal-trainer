# Logo Setup Instructions

## Save Your Logo

Please save the Christian Borja logo image as:

```
public/logo.png
```

The logo file should be:
- The green and white horizontal logo with the muscular arm/leaves icon
- Format: PNG (with transparent background preferred)
- Recommended dimensions: 800x200px or similar aspect ratio
- The logo shows:
  - Icon on the left (dark green with muscular arm and leaves)
  - "CHRISTIAN BORJA" in dark green
  - "HOLISTIC HEALTH & FITNESS" subtitle in lighter green

## Where the Logo is Used

The logo has been integrated into:

1. **Header** (components/Header.tsx)
   - Displays at 48px height on mobile
   - Displays at 56px height on desktop
   - Optimized with Next.js Image component

2. **Footer** (components/Footer.tsx)
   - Displays at 40px height
   - White background container for visibility on dark footer
